Welcome to St. Francis Senior Secondary School, Busunju

At St. Francis S.S.S. Busunju, we are dedicated to fostering a nurturing environment that promotes spiritual growth, academic excellence, and community service. Founded on the principles of faith and compassion exemplified by St. Francis of Assisi, our school strives to create a space where students can explore their spirituality, develop strong moral values, and engage meaningfully with the world around them

Learn More Our Classes

Enlightening Minds and Souls Through Knowledge and Faith

Here, we believe in nurturing the whole person—mind, body, and spirit. Our dedicated staff strives to create an environment that encourages curiosity, critical thinking, and a deep sense of community. We are passionate about empowering our students to become compassionate leaders who contribute positively to society. We invite you to explore our programs, engage with our community, and discover how St. Francis S.S.S. Busunju is shaping future generations in faith and knowledge. Together, let us walk the path of faith, friendship, and fellowship.
